The prince triceratops invites all the dinosaurs to a delicious veggie dinner.

Cinderella really wants to go because she's a vegetarian and her step-family are all carnivores and only let her eat meat.

Her step-sisters tease her as they put on their best herbivore costumes.

Her step-mother wants to marry off her daughters to the prince because he owns lots of luscious land where delicious animals go to graze and hide.

Her step-family locks her up and leaves to go to the feast. She cries one big dino tear that magically turns into a pterodactyl god mother.

The pterodactyl frees her and gives her a necklace to make her neck look shorter to hide from her family.

She goes to the ball and shares a textured soy protein meal with the prince and they fall in love...

but she has to run away at midnight. She drops her necklace.

The prince chases after and finds her necklace. The next day he tries to fit the necklace on all the lady dinos.

He finds Cinderella and they live happily ever after.
